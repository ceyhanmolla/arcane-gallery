Current Exhibition

Ephemeral Horizons

March 15 — June 28, 2026 · Works by Elara Voss

Elara Voss presents a haunting meditation on impermanence through large-scale compositions that blur the boundary between the organic and the synthetic. Her signature oil-and-resin technique creates luminous surfaces that shift with the viewer's position — each glance revealing a new stratum of depth.

This body of work, three years in the making, spans twelve monumental canvases and five smaller studies. Together they trace an arc from dusk to dawn, from dissolution to reconstitution.

About

The Gallery

Founded in 2012, Arcane has established itself as a leading voice in contemporary art, representing emerging and established artists whose work challenges convention and redefines the possible.

Our flagship space in the Arts District spans 8,000 square feet across two floors, with a dedicated project room for site-specific installations and a sculpture garden featuring large-scale works.
